About Royal Star

Mastering Freight Transport Across Egypt

Royal Star stands as a symbol of reliability and precision in land freight transportation. As a proud member of Orient Group, we leverage the strength of a diversified network comprising five specialized companies to deliver end-to-end transport solutions with unmatched efficiency.

With six years of presence in the Egyptian market, Royal Star has redefined how cargo moves across Egypt. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has positioned us as a leader in the logistics industry.

We specialize in container transport, heavy cargo, oversized equipment, and specialized trucking services, serving all major Egyptian ports with precision and care.

Our team of experienced professionals is trained in international standards of safety and logistics excellence, ensuring your cargo is in the best hands from pickup to delivery.

Our Services

Container Transport

Specialized transport for dry, reefer, and flat rack containers with precision handling and timely delivery to all Egyptian ports.

  • Dry container transportation
  • Refrigerated (reefer) container services
  • Flat rack container solutions
  • Port-to-door delivery

Heavy Cargo & Oversized Equipment

Expert handling and transportation for heavy and oversized cargo with specialized equipment and experienced personnel.

  • Heavy machinery transport
  • Oversized equipment handling
  • Specialized securing techniques
  • Route planning and permits

Low Bed Trucking

Specialized low bed trucking solutions for cargo that requires extra care and specific handling with minimal ground clearance.

  • Low bed trailer services
  • Extra-heavy load capacity
  • Hydraulic tilt mechanisms
  • Secure loading and unloading

Inland Freight Operations

Comprehensive freight services connecting all Egyptian ports with efficiency, reliability, and real-time tracking.

  • Nationwide coverage
  • Real-time cargo tracking
  • Multi-modal transport solutions
  • Customs clearance assistance

2024 Performance

Our Growing Footprint

In 2024, Royal Star successfully executed a total of 4,260 land freight trips across key Egyptian ports, demonstrating our growing footprint and operational strength.

Our strategic focus on major ports has enabled us to build a robust network that ensures timely and efficient cargo movement throughout Egypt.

With Alexandria as our primary operational hub, we've expanded our reach to cover Damietta and Sokhna, providing comprehensive coverage for all your logistics needs.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our performance metrics, with a 98% on-time delivery rate and 99.7% cargo safety record across all operations.
